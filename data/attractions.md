# Top Attractions in Japan

## Tokyo

### Senso-ji Temple

- Location: Asakusa
- Famous for traditional architecture and Nakamise Street

### Tokyo Skytree

- Height: 634 meters
- Observation decks with panoramic city views

### Shibuya Crossing

- World's busiest pedestrian intersection

### Meiji Shrine

- Peaceful shrine surrounded by forest

### Akihabara

- Anime, gaming, and electronics district

### teamLab Planets TOKYO

- Immersive digital art museum

## Kyoto

### Fushimi Inari Taisha

- Thousands of iconic red torii gates

### Kinkaku-ji

- The Golden Pavilion

### Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

- Scenic bamboo forest

### Kiyomizu-dera

- Historic temple with city views

## Osaka

### Dotonbori

- Food street and nightlife area

### Osaka Castle

- Historic landmark with museum

### Universal Studios Japan

- Theme park featuring Super Nintendo World

## Nara

### Nara Park

- Famous for free-roaming deer

### Todai-ji Temple

- Home to the Great Buddha statue

## Hiroshima

### Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park

- UNESCO World Heritage Site

### Miyajima Island

- Floating torii gate at Itsukushima Shrine

## Mount Fuji Area

### Lake Kawaguchiko

- Scenic views of Mount Fuji

### Chureito Pagoda

- One of Japan's most photographed locations

## Travel Tips

- Reserve popular attractions in advance.
- Visit major attractions early in the morning.
- Purchase tickets online whenever possible.
