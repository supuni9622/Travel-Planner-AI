# Accommodation Guide for Japan

## Types of Accommodation

### Hotels

- International hotel chains
- Business hotels
- Boutique hotels

Popular brands:

- APA Hotels
- Tokyu Stay
- Daiwa Roynet Hotels

### Ryokan

Traditional Japanese inns featuring:

- Tatami flooring
- Futon bedding
- Onsen baths
- Traditional meals

### Capsule Hotels

Suitable for:

- Solo travelers
- Short stays
- Budget-conscious visitors

### Hostels

- Shared dormitories
- Private rooms available
- Social atmosphere

## Average Nightly Costs

### Hostels

- JPY 3,000–6,000

### Budget Hotels

- JPY 7,000–12,000

### Mid-Range Hotels

- JPY 12,000–25,000

### Luxury Hotels

- JPY 30,000+

## Booking Tips

- Reserve accommodation 2–3 months in advance.
- Book even earlier during cherry blossom season.
- Check walking distance to train stations.
- Verify room sizes before booking.
- Confirm check-in and luggage storage policies.

## Popular Booking Platforms

- Booking.com
- Agoda
- Expedia
- Rakuten Travel

## Important Notes

- Check-in usually starts between 3:00 PM and 4:00 PM.
- Many hotels provide complimentary toiletries.
- Smoking and non-smoking rooms are strictly separated.
