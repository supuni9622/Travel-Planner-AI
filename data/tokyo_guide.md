# Tokyo Travel Guide

## About Tokyo

Tokyo is Japan's capital and the world's largest metropolitan area, blending ancient traditions with cutting-edge technology.

Best known for:

- Modern skyscrapers
- Historic temples
- Anime and gaming culture
- Exceptional public transportation
- Michelin-starred dining
- Shopping districts

## Best Time to Visit

### Spring (March–May)

- Cherry blossom season
- Mild temperatures
- Peak tourist season

### Summer (June–August)

- Hot and humid
- Festival season
- Higher accommodation prices

### Autumn (September–November)

- Comfortable weather
- Autumn foliage
- Ideal for sightseeing

### Winter (December–February)

- Cool and dry
- Fewer crowds
- Excellent seasonal illuminations

## Recommended Trip Duration

- First-time visitors: 5–7 days
- Tokyo only: 4–5 days
- Tokyo with nearby cities: 7–10 days

## Currency

- Currency: Japanese Yen (JPY)
- Credit cards are widely accepted, but cash is still useful.

## Language

- Official language: Japanese
- English signage is common in major tourist areas.

## Connectivity

- eSIM

## Essential Etiquette

- Queue patiently.
- Speak quietly on public transport.
- Remove shoes when required.
- Do not tip.
- Carry your trash until you find a bin.
